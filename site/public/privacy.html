[Your company name] ("we", "us") provides career support services, including CV feedback and job-search advice. This policy explains what personal data we collect through this website, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Eligibility

This service is intended for users aged 18 and over. We ask you to confirm this when you submit an enquiry. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18 through this form.

3. What we collect

  • Your name, email address, and phone number if provided
  • The role, industry, or programme you tell us you're interested in
  • Any message you write to us in the enquiry form
  • How you told us you heard about us
  • Your CV, and text extracted from it (skills, experience, education, and similar details it contains)

4. How we use it

  • To generate an automatic, AI-assisted read on your CV, shown to you immediately after you submit it
  • To let our staff review your enquiry and CV and follow up with you personally
  • To send you a confirmation email acknowledging your enquiry

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to third parties.

5. Who else sees it

Both act as data processors on our instructions and do not use your data for their own purposes. [Confirm this against each provider's current terms before publishing.]
